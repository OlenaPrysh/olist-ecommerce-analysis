# Olist E-Commerce Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist. The goal of the analysis is to understand customer behavior, revenue trends, payment preferences, product performance, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

The project was completed as part of a Data Analytics portfolio and focuses on answering business questions using Python, Pandas, and data visualization.

## Dataset

The dataset used in this project is the **Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist**, available on Kaggle.

Source:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce

It contains information about:

* orders
* customers
* payments
* products
* product categories
* order items
* delivery dates
* customer reviews

The data covers orders from 2016 to 2018.

## Business Questions

The analysis answers the following questions:

1. How has revenue changed over time?
2. How has the number of orders changed over time?
3. Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
4. Which product categories have high sales but low customer ratings?
5. Does delivery performance affect customer satisfaction?
6. What percentage of orders are delivered on time?
7. What payment methods do customers prefer?
8. How many customers make repeat purchases?
9. Which states generate the highest revenue?
10. Which product categories receive the highest and lowest customer ratings?

## Key Findings

* Revenue and order volume increased significantly during the analyzed period.
* Health & Beauty, Watches & Gifts, and Bed Bath Table were among the highest revenue-generating categories.
* Some high-revenue categories had below-average customer ratings, which indicates opportunities for improving customer experience.
* Delivery performance has a strong relationship with customer satisfaction.
* About 90.7% of orders were delivered on time or earlier.
* Repeat purchase rate was low, showing an opportunity to improve customer retention.
* Credit cards were the most commonly used payment method.

## Recommendations

* Improve customer experience in high-revenue but lower-rated categories.
* Continue investing in logistics to maintain a high on-time delivery rate.
* Analyze customer review comments to understand the reasons behind low ratings.
* Develop customer retention strategies such as loyalty programs and personalized offers.
* Focus marketing efforts on top-performing categories and high-revenue states.
* Monitor customer satisfaction together with revenue metrics.
